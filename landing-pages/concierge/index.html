Common questions

Before you start.

Is this really a one-time payment?
Yes. $500 once, no recurring subscription. Your Success Manager stays assigned to you until you land a job — there is no fixed time limit and no additional charge for that ongoing support.
What exactly does my Success Manager do?
They rebuild your resume, manage and submit your Auto-Apply applications, run 1-on-1 interview prep sessions with you, and keep your entire Lightforth dashboard organised — checking in with you regularly with updates.
What is the 3 months of Lightforth Premium for?
It's the platform layer underneath the service — Resume Builder, Auto-Apply, Interview Prep, and Copilot, all included free for your first 3 months alongside your Success Manager.
What happens after the 3 months of Premium ends?
Your Success Manager continues working your search regardless. If you'd like to keep platform access beyond the included 3 months, you can subscribe separately at standard Lightforth pricing — this is entirely optional and not required for your Success Manager to keep helping you.
Is this for students, professionals, or both?
Both. Whether you're a student with no experience, a recent graduate, or an experienced professional who simply doesn't have time to job hunt, your Success Manager adapts the approach to where you are.
How quickly do I get matched with a Success Manager?
Within 24 hours of your payment going through, you'll be introduced to your assigned Success Manager and your first intake call will be scheduled.
