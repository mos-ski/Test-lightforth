## 3. Getting Started — Web vs. Stealth

There are two versions of Interview Copilot depending on how your interview is set up.

### Web Version (Browser-based)
Best for: **Google Meet, Zoom web, or any browser-based interview**

- No download needed — works directly in your browser
- During setup, you share only your **interview tab + audio** (not your full screen)
- Copilot mirrors the interview in its canvas, or you can use picture-in-picture mode
- **Important limitation:** If the interviewer asks you to share your full screen, Copilot will be visible — you'll need to quit it quickly in that case
- For those situations, use the Stealth version below

### Stealth Version (Desktop App)
Best for: **Zoom app, Microsoft Teams, Slack, and any non-browser interview platform**

This version is fully invisible — even if you're asked to share your full screen, Copilot cannot be seen by the interviewer.

**Download:** Go to the Downloads page on Lightforth and choose your version:
- **Windows:** Download the Windows installer
- **Mac:** Check your Mac type first
  - Click the Apple icon (top-left of screen) → "About This Mac"
  - If it says Intel → download the Intel version
  - If it says Apple M1/M2/M3 (M-series) → download the Apple Silicon version

**Setup after download:**
1. Move the Lightforth Copilot app into your **Applications folder**
2. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security
3. Grant **Screen Recording** and **System Audio** access to Lightforth Copilot
4. If Lightforth Copilot doesn't appear in the permissions list:
   - Click the **"+"** button
   - Navigate to your Downloads or Applications folder and select Lightforth Copilot
5. The app will restart automatically — audio will connect correctly after restart

## 4. Live Walkthrough (Step by Step)

> Walk through this on screen while narrating.

**Step 1 — Landing Page**
- Open Interview Copilot from the sidebar
- Point out the history table of past sessions
- Say: *"Every session is saved so you can go back, review your transcript, and get better each time."*

**Step 2 — Start a Session**
- Click **"+ Start Interview"**
- The setup screen opens

**Step 3 — Setup**
- Enter a job title (use a suggestion: "UI/UX Designer" or "Software Engineer")
- For resume: select **"Use Lightforth Resume"** (point out the RECOMMENDED badge)
  - This means Copilot tailors answers to YOUR background, not generic responses
- Optionally paste a job description for even more targeted answers
- Say: *"The more context you give it, the better the answers it generates"*

**Step 4 — Connect Audio**
- Click the audio dropdown and select your microphone
- Click **"Enable Microphone Access"**
- Say: *"This is how Copilot hears the interview — it listens through your mic"*

**Step 5 — Choose Response Style**
- Click **Continue** — the Preference modal appears
- Show the 3 response types:
  - **Default** — Full, natural-sounding answer ready to read out
  - **Headlines** — Structured bullet points (STAR format) to hit every key point
  - **Coaching** — Brief tips and pointers to guide your own answer
- Say: *"Pick the style that matches how you like to speak in interviews"*
- Select one and click **Next**

**Step 6 — Live Interview Screen**
- The dark interview canvas opens
- Point out the two panels:
  - **Left panel (Live Interview Response):** This is where Copilot's suggested answer appears in real time as the interviewer speaks
  - **Right panel top (Your Interview):** Shows your interview screen (the tab you shared)
  - **Right panel bottom (Your Transcript):** Captures what you and the interviewer are saying
- Show the **canvas layout presets** at the top (Balanced, Response, Mirror, Transcript) — users can resize panels to fit their screen setup
- Click **"Share Interview Screen"** → then **"Start Interview"**
- Timer starts — the red dot shows Copilot is active and listening

**Step 7 — End & Review**
- Click **"End Interview"**
- Complete screen confirms the session is saved
- Click through to the **Report** — show the transcript on the left and AI insights on the right
- Say: *"After every session, Lightforth tells you exactly what to improve for next time"*

## 5. Selling Points

### 1. Real-time answers
Copilot listens and responds as the interviewer speaks. No delay, no typing, no searching your memory.

### 2. Two modes — Web and Stealth
- **Web:** Zero setup, works in the browser for online interview platforms
- **Stealth:** Desktop app that is completely invisible even during a full screen share — perfect for Zoom, Teams, and Slack interviews

### 3. Three response styles
Default (full answer), Headlines (bullet-point structure), or Coaching (tips to guide yourself). You choose what matches your style.

### 4. Answers tailored to your resume
Copilot uses your actual Lightforth resume to generate answers about your real experience — not generic filler.

### 5. Post-interview insights
Every session gives you a full transcript and AI feedback on clarity, structure, and what to improve. You get better with every interview.
