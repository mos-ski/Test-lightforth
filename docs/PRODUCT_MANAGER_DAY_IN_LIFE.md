# Day in the Life: Product Manager (PM) @ Lightforth

The PM at Lightforth (currently **Adedamola Adewale**) is the "Engine Room" of the 8-week sprint. Your role is the bridge between technical execution (Agents/AI) and the 45 students' success.

---

## Morning: Sprint Alignment & Engineering Syncs
*   **08:30 AM – The "Pulse" Check:** Review the **Project Timeline**. Are we on track for the **Copilot Core** launch in Week 2?
*   **09:30 AM – Agent Swarm & Marketing Sync:** On Wednesdays, you meet with Marketing to audit the CAC ($31). On other days, you sync with the Browser Automation Engineer.
*   **10:30 AM – Engineering Talk & Test:** 
    *   **Tuesdays:** 1:1 with **Front-End** and **Full-Stack** engineers to test the latest mobile/web builds.
    *   **Wed/Fri:** Product Demos & QA sessions.

## Mid-Morning: Strategic Problem Solving
*   **11:30 AM – PRD Refinement:** You update the **Mobile App PRD**. Based on student feedback, you decide to prioritize the **"See Replay"** feature earlier in the tracker.
*   **01:00 PM – The "Student Success" Bridge:** You meet with Career Specialists to translate student needs into technical tickets.

## 02:00 PM – 05:00 PM: The CST Executive Window
*Focus: Syncing with US-based stakeholders during their morning hours.*

*   **Daily @ 03:00 PM:** 15-minute written update to the **Chief of Staff (COS)**.
*   **Executive syncs:**
    *   **Mondays:** **SSM Senior Manager Sync** to review student callback rates.
    *   **Tuesdays:** **Design Review** for new product visuals.
    *   **Wednesdays:** **CVO Deep Dive** (Bi-weekly) to align on strategic maneuvers.
    *   **Thursdays:** **Ops & Support Sync** to address payment failures (78% success rate).
    *   **Fridays:** **Head of Product Strategy** and **Weekly Stakeholder Report**.

---

## Core Focus Areas:
1.  **Velocity**: Keeping the 8-week "Copilot-First" roadmap moving.
2.  **Quality**: Ensuring the Agent Swarm and Copilot aren't just "functional" but "premium."
3.  **Timezone Synergy**: Maximizing the 2pm-5pm overlap with the CST-based executive team.
4.  **Growth**: Relentlessly pursuing the **OKR O2** (Onboarding Completion) and **OKR O3** (Paywall Conversion).
