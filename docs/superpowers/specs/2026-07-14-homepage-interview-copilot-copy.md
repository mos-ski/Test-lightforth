# Lightforth Homepage Copy — Interview Copilot Repositioning

**Status:** draft, hero headline pending decision
**Scope:** copy only — layout/implementation happens in a follow-up pass
**Replaces:** the current "/" homepage (career-acceleration-generalist framing)

## Context

The current homepage sells Lightforth as a general career-acceleration platform
(resume + auto-apply + interview prep). This rewrite repositions the homepage
around the **Interview Copilot** as the lead product, following a new visual
layout the user has already mocked up (tabbed panel, 3-stat strip, authority
photo section, 6-icon feature grid, three product deep-dives, dark CTA banner).
The mockup's original copy is a placeholder from an unrelated template and is
being fully replaced.

Competitive research (Final Round AI, Parakeet AI) shows both lead hard on
**"real-time, undetectable answers during live interviews"** and back it with
speed/accuracy stats. Neither positions itself as a full career platform —
that's Lightforth's actual differentiator: resume → auto-apply → interview
prep → live copilot → meeting copilot, one thread through the whole job
search, not just the interview moment.

## Nav

Solutions · Product · Resources · Pricing · Login · Get Started *(unchanged)*

## Hero — headline pending decision

Ten directions drafted, spanning different angles:

1. "What's your next interview really worth?" — stakes-framed, closest to the original mockup instinct
2. "Never freeze in an interview again." — direct benefit, competitor-style confidence
3. "The only AI that's with you from resume to offer." — full-journey differentiator
4. "Your AI Copilot for every interview." — plain, category-defining
5. "Answers in real time. Invisible on every call." — leads with the "undetectable" hook competitors sell hardest
6. "Say the right thing, every time." — outcome-framed, conversational
7. "Walk into every interview already prepared to win it." — confidence/outcome
8. "Interviews are won in real time. So is Lightforth." — punchy, editorial
9. "From your resume to your next 'yes.'" — journey-framed, shorter/warmer than #3
10. "The AI that's actually in the room with you." — presence/immediacy angle

**Recommendation:** #3 or #9 (full-journey angle) — the one claim neither
Final Round nor Parakeet can make, since both are interview-moment-only tools.

CTAs: "Download Now" (desktop copilot) + "Get Started for Free"
