# Lightforth — Company Overview

*HR & Recruitment Reference · Growth Stage · US Market · June 2026*

---

## What is Lightforth?

Lightforth is an AI-powered career  platform designed to help job seekers in the United States land roles faster and more confidently. It automates and enhances every stage of the job search — from building the right resume, to preparing for interviews, to applying to jobs on the candidate's behalf.

**Core promise: "Let's get you hired."**

Lightforth is a web-based product available at lightforth.ai. It operates on a freemium model with a free tier and a paid Pro plan.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Job searching is one of the most stressful, repetitive, and inefficient experiences most people face. Lightforth was built to fix that.

- Generic resumes that get filtered out before a human ever reads them
- Hours wasted manually applying to dozens of jobs with little feedback
- No structured way to prepare for interviews or know what to expect
- Candidates showing up underprepared because real-time support doesn't exist

---

## What Lightforth Does

Lightforth combines four intelligent tools into one platform, covering the full arc of the job search journey.

---

### 1. Resume Builder

AI tailors the user's resume to any specific job description, then scores it against ATS (Applicant Tracking System) filters — so it gets seen by real people, not just filtered out. The final resume can be downloaded as a PDF, Word document, or plain text.

**How it works:**

1. Upload an existing resume or build one from scratch inside Lightforth
2. Paste the target job description
3. AI tailors the resume and scores it against ATS filters
4. Download in the preferred format

---

### 2. Auto-Apply

Users define their target roles, preferred locations, and job preferences once. Lightforth then finds matching open positions and submits applications on the user's behalf — removing the manual effort of applying to dozens of jobs individually.

**How it works:**

1. Set target roles, location, and preferences
2. Attach the preferred resume
3. Review matched jobs
4. Lightforth applies automatically

---

### 3. Interview Prep

An AI-powered mock interview simulator that knows the role the candidate is targeting. After every session, the user receives a full performance report with feedback on how they did and where to improve.

**How it works:**

1. Select a resume
2. Set the job details and interview type
3. Practice with the AI simulator
4. Receive a performance report

---

### 4. Interview Copilot

Real-time AI support during a live interview. The candidate shares their interview audio and the AI listens to the conversation, surfacing suggested answers in real time — right when they're needed.

**How it works:**

1. Connect audio to the live interview
2. AI listens in real time
3. Answer suggestions appear on screen as the interview progresses
4. The candidate stays in full control throughout
