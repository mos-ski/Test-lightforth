# Day in the Life: Business Intelligence (BI) Analyst @ Lightforth

Based on the current infrastructure and Q2 2026 OKRs, a BI Analyst at Lightforth is the "Guardian of the Funnel." Your primary mission is to bridge the gap between our high-volume automation (Agent Swarm) and actual business growth.

---

## Morning: Health Check & Revenue Monitoring
*   **09:00 AM – The "War Room" Dashboard:** Audit the **Overview** dashboard. You start by checking the **Paid Users (281/500)** and **MRR**. Because we operate across NGN and USD, you ensure the currency conversions and payout reconciliations are accurate.
*   **09:30 AM – Payment Failure Investigation:** Our current **Payment Failure Rate is 22%** (Target: 8%). You slice this data by region and provider (Flutterwave vs. Stripe) to identify if there are specific bank downtimes or friction in the checkout flow.

## Mid-Morning: Funnel & Activation Deep-Dive
*   **10:30 AM – Onboarding Friction Analysis:** The **Onboarding Completion is at 42%** (Target: 70%). You perform a drop-off analysis to see if users are getting stuck at the **Resume Upload** or **Job Preferences** step.
*   **11:30 AM – Time to First Value (TTFV):** You analyze why the TTFV is **12 minutes** when our target is **< 5 minutes**. Is the AI response latency (current 4s) causing the delay? You sync with the Engineering team to prioritize infrastructure fixes for O4.

## Lunch: Stakeholder Sync
*   **01:00 PM – Student Success Reporting:** You provide a summary report for the Student Success Managers. You show which of the **45 students** are seeing the highest **Callback Conversion (Goal: 5%)** and which need their resumes retailored because their success rate is dipping.

## Afternoon: Growth & Experimentation
*   **02:00 PM – CAC vs. Acquisition Efficiency:** Our **CAC is currently $31** (Target: $18). You analyze the **Paid Ad Conversion (2.1%)** vs. **Landing Page Conversion (9%)** to recommend shifting budget from underperforming Facebook campaigns to higher-converting influencer channels.
*   **03:30 PM – Experiment Validation (OKR O7):** You review the results of the latest A/B test on the **Paywall Conversion (current 2.0%, Target 6.0%)**. You validate if the "annual discount" experiment reached a statistically significant sample of 500 users within the 7-day window.

## Evening: Forecasting & Strategy
*   **05:00 PM – Q3 Forecasting:** Based on the current **Retention Rate (14% returning users)**, you build a forecast for the CVO showing when we will hit the 500 paid user milestone.
*   **06:00 PM – End of Day Report:** A concise Slack summary to the CVO and PM (Adedamola) highlighting the daily **Checkout Drop-off (49%)** and any infrastructure incidents reported that day.

---

## Core KPIs you own:
1.  **Revenue**: MRR growth and Freemium-to-Paid conversion.
2.  **Activation**: TTFV and Onboarding completion.
3.  **Efficiency**: CAC and Payment Success rates.
4.  **Product Quality**: AI Latency and Platform Uptime impact on revenue.
