# Lightforth — Security & Privacy Overview

## Security Philosophy

Lightforth handles people's resumes, job applications, and live interview audio — some of the most personal material in someone's career. We treat it that way.

---

## Certifications & Compliance

### GDPR & CCPA
- **Status:** Compliant
- **Consumer rights supported:** access, export, deletion, opt-out
- **We do not sell customer data.**

### Data Residency
- US-based hosting (AWS)

## Data Security

### Encryption
| Layer | Method |
|---|---|
| In transit | TLS 1.3 |
| At rest | AES-256 |

### Access Controls
- Multi-factor authentication available on all accounts
- Role-based access for coach/organization admin dashboards (bundle plans)

---

## Data Privacy

### What We Collect
- Account info: name, email
- Resume and application content (customer-owned)
- Interview audio, processed live for Copilot suggestions
- Anonymized usage analytics

### What We Don't Collect
- Payment card data (processed by Stripe — never touches our servers)
- Anything beyond what's needed to run the product

### Data Ownership
- **You own your data.** Export anytime.
- Cancel and your data is retained 30 days (for export), then deleted unless reactivated.

---

## Interview Audio — The Question People Actually Ask

### "Is my interview audio recorded and stored?"
Audio is processed in real time to generate Copilot's suggestions. It is **not persisted** unless you explicitly enable session recording in settings. If you do enable it, recordings are retained per your own retention setting and can be deleted anytime.

### "Can the interviewer tell I'm using Copilot?"
No. Suggestions render only on the candidate's own screen. There is no signal visible to anyone else on the call.

---

## AI & Data Usage

- **We do not use your resume, application, or interview data to train AI models.**
- AI suggestions are generated in real time via third-party AI providers under strict data-processing agreements — no persistent model training occurs on customer content.
- You can review any AI-generated suggestion or resume rewrite; nothing is applied without your action (Auto-Apply always shows you the match list before submitting).

---

## Coach & Organization Bundles

- Bundle admins can see seat activation status and aggregate usage (e.g., "18 of 20 seats active"), but **cannot see an individual's resume, applications, or interview content.** Coaching happens outside the platform; Lightforth doesn't expose personal job-search material to the org admin.
