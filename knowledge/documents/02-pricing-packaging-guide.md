# Lightforth — Pricing & Packaging Guide

## Pricing Philosophy

Lightforth prices per person, not per seat-license bureaucracy. It's designed to be:
- **Simple** — two plans, one clear upgrade path
- **Low-commitment** — monthly by default, cancel anytime
- **Justifiable in one sentence** — cheaper than one hour of a career coach's time, and available every day

## What "credits" mean

A credit is roughly one AI action — one tailored resume rewrite, one Auto-Apply submission, one mock interview question, or one minute of live Copilot coaching. Most active job seekers use 30-60 credits/month; Premium's 100 gives real headroom for a heavy application season.

## Discount Guidelines

### Standard Discounts
| Commitment | Discount | Approval Required |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly | 0% | — |
| Annual | 20% (baked into the $39/$63 annual price) | None — always available |

### Special Situations
| Scenario | Discount | Approval Required |
|---|---|---|
| Student (valid .edu email) | 15% off any plan | None — self-serve |
| Referral (existing customer code) | 1 month free for both | None — self-serve |
| Career coach / bootcamp bundle, 5-9 seats | 15% off per seat | Sales Lead |
| Career coach / bootcamp bundle, 10-19 seats | 20% off per seat | Sales Lead |
| Career coach / bootcamp bundle, 20+ seats | 25% off per seat | Founder/CVO |
| Non-profit workforce program | Up to 30% off bundle | Founder/CVO |

### Stacking Rules
- Student and referral discounts don't stack with each other — apply whichever is larger
- Bundle discounts are per-contract, not combinable with individual student/referral discounts
- Nothing stacks past 30% off list

## Value Math (use in a demo, not a spreadsheet)

The honest version: this isn't an ROI-calculator sale. The framing that actually lands is cost-per-outcome:

> "Premium is $79 a month. If it gets you one offer even two weeks sooner than you'd have gotten it on your own, that's easily worth more than two months of the subscription — and you're not locked into anything longer than a month."

For a coach/bootcamp buyer, the framing is capacity, not ROI:
> "You can't personally run a live mock interview with all 40 of your students the week before their real interviews. Copilot can, for all of them, at the same time."

## Common Pricing Questions

### "Why is Premium $30 more than Pro?"
Meeting Copilot (for informational interviews, panel interviews, and post-offer negotiation calls) plus double the credits — most people who upgrade do it during a heavy interview stretch, then can downgrade back to Pro after.

### "Can I switch plans?"
Yes, anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### "Is there a free trial?"
Yes — 14 days, full feature access, no credit card required.

### "What happens to my resumes/data if I cancel?"
Everything is retained for 30 days after cancellation so you can export it, then deleted unless you reactivate.

### "Do you offer payment plans for the coach/bootcamp bundles?"
Bundles under 20 seats are billed upfront annually. 20+ seat bundles can be split into two payments (50% upfront, 50% at 60 days) — Founder/CVO approval required.
