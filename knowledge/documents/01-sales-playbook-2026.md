# Lightforth — Sales Playbook 2026

## Overview

Lightforth is an AI-powered career platform: Resume Builder, Auto-Apply, Interview Prep, and Interview Copilot. This playbook covers how to position, demo, and close individual job seekers — and organizations buying seats on their behalf — on Lightforth Pro and Premium.

---

## Product Positioning

### One-Liner
"Lightforth gets you hired faster — an AI resume that beats the ATS, auto-apply that does the busywork, and a live Copilot that whispers the right answer in your ear during the actual interview."

### Value Proposition
- **Tailored resume in minutes** — matched to the exact job description, optimized for ATS
- **Auto-Apply** — stop spending nights filling out the same form fifty times
- **Realistic mock interviews** with a full performance report after every session
- **Interview Copilot** — real-time suggested answers during the live interview, invisible to the interviewer

### Key Differentiators
1. **Real-time, not just prep** — Interview Copilot works live, during the actual interview, not just in mock practice
2. **One platform, whole funnel** — resume → apply → prep → live interview, no juggling four different tools
3. **Undetectable to the interviewer** — Copilot only appears on the candidate's own screen
4. **Fast to value** — a tailored resume and your first Auto-Apply batch inside 15 minutes of signing up

## Who We're Selling To

### Primary: The Individual Job Seeker
- **Who:** Active job seekers — recent grads, career changers, people between roles, people trying to move up
- **Pain:** Applications go into a black hole, interviews feel like guessing, no time to tailor a resume for every posting
- **Goal:** Land more interviews, walk into interviews confident, get the offer faster
- **What they care about:** "Will this actually get me hired," "is it worth $49-79/month," "is this cheating"

### Secondary: Coaches & Organizations Buying for Others
- **Who:** Career coaches, coding/UX bootcamps, university career centers, outplacement/severance programs
- **Pain:** They're responsible for a cohort's job-search outcomes but can't personally coach interview technique for 30+ people at once
- **Goal:** Give every person they support a tool that reinforces good interview technique between coaching sessions
- **What they care about:** Bulk pricing, seat management, whether it complements (not replaces) their own coaching

## Sales Process

### 1. Discovery (10-15 min)
**Goal:** Understand where they are in the job search and what's actually stuck.

**Key Questions (individual):**
- Where are you in the search — just starting, or already interviewing?
- What's the biggest bottleneck: not enough interviews, or not converting the interviews you get?
- Have you customized your resume for each application, or sending one version everywhere?
- How many interviews have you had in the last month? How did they go?

**Key Questions (coach/organization):**
- How many people are you supporting right now?
- What's the gap between your coaching sessions — what do people need help with in between?
- Do you already recommend any tools today?

### 2. Demo (15-25 min)
**Demo Flow:**
1. **Resume tailoring** (5 min) — paste a real job description, show the tailored resume + ATS score
2. **Auto-Apply** (3 min) — show the match list and one-click apply, with full visibility before anything is submitted
3. **Interview Prep** (5 min) — run one mock question, show the performance report
4. **Interview Copilot — the moment** (7 min) — this is the close. Simulate a live interview question and show the suggested answer appear in real time, invisible to the "interviewer"
5. **Q&A** (5 min)

**Demo Tips:**
- Use THEIR actual target job title and a real job posting they send you — generic demos don't land
- Let the silence sit after the Copilot suggestion appears on screen — it's the moment that sells itself
- If they ask "is this cheating," answer it head-on before they have to (see Objection Handling)

### 3. Trial
**Structure:** 14-day free trial, full feature access, no credit card required.
**What to tell them to do:** Tailor one resume, run at least one Auto-Apply batch, complete one full mock interview before the trial ends — that's usually enough to see real value.

### 4. Close

**Common Objections:**
- "It's too expensive" → reframe on cost-per-interview and cost-per-offer, not monthly price
- "I can write my own resume" → it's not about writing, it's about matching the exact keywords an ATS is scanning for, for every single posting
- "Isn't this cheating in the interview?" → answer directly, don't dodge it
- "I already tried [Teal / Kickresume]" → they don't do live, real-time interview coaching — that's the actual gap
- "I need to think about it" → propose the 14-day trial as the "thinking" step, not a separate decision later

## Objection Handling (Quick Reference)

### "It's too expensive"
"Let's do the math a different way — what's one job worth to you? If Premium helps you land the offer even two weeks sooner, it's already paid for itself many times over. And you can cancel anytime."

### "Isn't using AI in an interview cheating?"
"Copilot doesn't put words in your mouth you don't mean — it surfaces the relevant point from your own resume and experience, fast, in the moment nerves make you blank. Most people don't fail interviews because they don't know the answer — they fail because they freeze. This fixes the freeze."

### "I already tried [competitor]"
"What was missing?" — nine times out of ten it's that the other tool stopped at resume/prep and never followed them into the actual live interview. That's the one thing only Lightforth does.

### "I need to think about it"
"Totally fair. The 14-day trial is free and doesn't need a card — would it make more sense to just try it on your next real application instead of deciding in the abstract?"
