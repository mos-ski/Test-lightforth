# Lightforth — Customer Case Studies

## Case Study 1: Career Changer into Product Management

### Profile
- **Background:** 6 years in operations, no formal PM title, applying for Product Manager roles
- **Plan:** Premium
- **Timeline:** 7 weeks from signup to offer

### The Challenge
Strong experience, but a resume that read as "operations," not "product." Applications were going out with the same generic resume to every posting, and the two interviews she landed both stalled after the first round.

### What She Did
Used Resume Builder to re-tailor her resume per job description — reframing operations work in product-outcome language matched to each posting's keywords. Ran Auto-Apply against a filtered list of 40 target roles over three weeks. Used Interview Prep for behavioral-question practice, then Interview Copilot live during two follow-up interviews.

### The Result
- Interview rate went from roughly 1 in 20 applications to 1 in 8 after resume retailoring
- Received an offer in week 7, at 12% above her stated target salary

### Key Quote
> "The resume rewrite alone changed everything — recruiters started replying within days instead of never. But Copilot in the actual interview is what got me through the panel round where I used to freeze."

## Case Study 3: Coaching Cohort — Regional Coding Bootcamp

### Profile
- **Organization:** A 12-week coding bootcamp, ~35 graduates per cohort
- **Plan:** Premium bundle, 20 seats (25% off)
- **Timeline:** Ongoing, per cohort

### The Challenge
Career coaches could run one mock interview per graduate before graduation — not enough repetition for people who'd never interviewed for a technical role before. Placement rates lagged behind the bootcamp's own targets.

### What They Did
Gave every graduating student a Premium seat two weeks before graduation. Coaches used their own sessions for strategy and encouragement; Lightforth's Interview Prep and Copilot handled the volume of repeated practice and live interview support coaches couldn't personally scale to.

### The Result
- Cohort placement rate within 90 days of graduation rose from 61% to 74% over two cohorts
- Career coaches reported spending less time on repeat mock-interview requests and more time on individual strategy

### Key Quote
> "We can't be in the room for 35 real interviews in the same two weeks. Copilot can be, for all of them, at once."
>
> — Career Services Lead
