# Lightforth — Frequently Asked Questions

## General Product Questions

### What is Lightforth?
Lightforth is an AI-powered career platform that helps job seekers land roles faster. It combines four tools: Resume Builder, Auto-Apply, Interview Prep, and Interview Copilot.

### Who is Lightforth for?
Active job seekers in the United States — recent grads, career changers, and people making a move. Also used by career coaches, bootcamps, and university career centers who buy seat bundles for the people they support.

### How does Lightforth work?
AI tailors your resume to a specific job description, automatically applies to matching positions (with your approval), simulates interview practice, and provides real-time coaching during the live interview itself.

### Is Lightforth free?
There's a free tier with limited credits. Pro ($49/mo) and Premium ($79/mo) unlock higher usage and the live desktop Copilot.

## Resume Builder Questions

### How does the AI resume tailoring work?
Upload your resume and paste the target job description. AI matches your experience to the keywords and requirements in that specific posting — the version you get is different for every job, not a single generic resume.

### What is ATS scoring?
A score showing how well your resume will perform against automated screening systems (ATS), with specific recommendations to close the gap.

### What formats can I download my resume in?
PDF, Word, or plain text — all ATS-compatible.

### Can I create multiple versions of my resume?
Yes, unlimited on Pro and Premium. We recommend a fresh tailored version per application.

## Auto-Apply Questions

### How does Auto-Apply work?
Set your target roles, locations, and preferences once. Lightforth finds matching open positions and submits applications for you.

### Will I know which jobs Lightforth applies to?
Yes — every match is shown to you before submission. Nothing goes out without your review.

### Can I customize my application for each job?
Yes — review and edit resume and materials before each submission.

### How many jobs can I apply to?
Depends on your credit balance. Pro's 50 credits/month and Premium's 100 both cover a realistic active-search volume; heavy months may need a credit top-up.

## Interview Prep Questions

### How does the AI mock interview work?
Select your resume and target job, then practice with the AI interviewer. You get a full performance report after every session.

### What types of interviews can I practice?
Behavioral, technical, case study, and general — the AI adapts to your target role.

### Can I practice with a specific company in mind?
Yes — set the job details for any company and the AI tailors questions and feedback accordingly.

## Interview Copilot Questions

### What is Interview Copilot?
Real-time AI support during the actual live interview. The candidate shares interview audio (via browser or desktop app); the AI listens and surfaces suggested answers on-screen, right when they're needed.

### Is this cheating?
Interview Copilot is a coaching tool, not a script. It surfaces the relevant point from your own resume and experience faster than nerves let you recall it on your own — it doesn't invent an experience you don't have.

### Can the interviewer see my Copilot?
No. Suggestions appear only on your own screen.

### Does Copilot work for phone interviews, or only video?
Both — audio can come from the desktop app's system audio, a browser tab, or a phone call routed through the desktop app's microphone input.

## Coach & Organization Bundle Questions

### How does seat pricing work for a bundle?
5-9 seats: 15% off. 10-19 seats: 20% off. 20+ seats: 25% off. All bundles billed annually to the organization.

### Can I see what my students/clients are doing in the app?
You can see seat activation and aggregate usage (e.g., how many seats are active), but not an individual's resume, applications, or interview content — that stays private to them.

### Can seats be reassigned?
Yes — an unused or inactive seat can be reassigned to someone else at any time at no extra cost.

### Do bundle members get the same features as a regular Premium subscriber?
Yes, identical feature access — the bundle only changes who pays and how seats are managed.

## Account & Billing Questions

### How do I upgrade my plan?
Account → Billing → select new plan. Changes take effect immediately.

### Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, anytime. Monthly plans end at the close of the current billing period; annual plans end at the close of the annual term.

### Do you offer refunds?
14-day money-back guarantee on new subscriptions. See `knowledge-center/01-refund-policy.md`.

### Can I change my plan mid-cycle?
Yes — upgrades are immediate and prorated; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers does Lightforth support?
Latest Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Chrome is recommended.

### Is there a mobile app?
Yes, iOS and Android, supporting Resume Builder, Auto-Apply, and Interview Prep. Interview Copilot requires the desktop app for audio sharing.

Modern browser, stable internet, a microphone (for Copilot), and the desktop app (Mac or Windows) for live interview coaching.

## Privacy & Security Questions

### Is my data secure?
Yes — encrypted at rest and in transit, GDPR/CCPA compliant. We do not sell your data.

### Do you use my data to train AI?
No. AI suggestions are generated in real time using third-party providers under strict data-processing agreements — your content is never used to train models.

### Is my interview audio recorded?
Only if you explicitly enable session recording. By default, audio is processed live for Copilot and not persisted.

### Can I delete my data?
Yes, anytime — full account and data deletion, processed within 30 days.
