# Lightforth — Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Policy

### 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee
- New subscribers can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked
- Applies to the initial subscription charge only — not to add-on credit top-ups
- One 14-day guarantee per customer (not renewable on every re-subscribe)

### How to Request a Refund
1. Email support@lightforth.ai within 14 days of purchase
2. Include account email (reason is optional but helps us improve)
3. Processed within 5-7 business days, returned to the original payment method

### What Happens After Refund
- Account downgraded to the free tier
- Data retained 30 days (exportable), then deleted unless reactivated

---

## Subscription Cancellations

### Monthly Plans
- Cancel anytime from account settings
- Access continues until the end of the current billing period, no partial refund

### Annual Plans
- Cancel anytime, effective at end of the annual term
- No mid-term refund outside the 14-day guarantee

### Coach & Organization Bundles
- Bundles are billed annually per the signed order; cancellation follows the same 14-day guarantee window from the bundle's start date
- After 14 days, cancellation is effective at the end of the annual term; individual seats can still be reassigned within an active bundle at any time

---

## Exceptions

### Billing Errors
- Contact support immediately for any charge you believe is in error — full refund, no time limit

### Extended Service Outage
- Pro-rated credit for a confirmed extended outage affecting your usage

### After the 14-Day Window
- Standard cancellation policy applies; we still want the feedback at support@lightforth.ai
