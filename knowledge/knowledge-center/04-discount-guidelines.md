# Lightforth — Discount Guidelines

## Overview
Consistent, fair discounting across every conversation. Lead with value first — discounts are a tool, not the opening move.

---

## Self-Serve Discounts (No Approval Needed)

| Scenario | Discount | How |
|---|---|---|
| Annual billing | 20% (Pro $39/mo, Premium $63/mo) | Automatic at checkout |
| Student (valid .edu email) | 15% off any plan | Automatic with .edu verification |
| Referral (existing customer's code) | 1 month free for both parties | Applied via referral code at signup |

---

## Bundle Discounts (Coach / Organization Buyers)

| Seats | Discount | Approval |
|---|---|---|
| 5-9 | 15% off per seat | Sales Lead |
| 10-19 | 20% off per seat | Sales Lead |
| 20+ | 25% off per seat | Founder/CVO |
| Verified non-profit workforce program | Up to 30% off | Founder/CVO |

**Documentation required for any bundle discount:** organization name, seat count, contract term (always annual), and — for non-profit rate — 501(c)(3) or equivalent verification.

---

## Discount Request Process (Bundles Only)

### Step 1: Determine Tier
Based on seat count per the table above.

### Step 2: Prepare Request
- Organization name and contact
- Seat count and requested discount
- One-line justification (e.g., "12-seat bootcamp cohort, annual")

### Step 3: Submit
- **5-19 seats:** Slack/email to Sales Lead, approved within 24 hours
- **20+ seats or non-profit rate:** Request form to Founder/CVO, approved within 48 hours

### Step 4: Follow Up
Confirm approval before quoting a final number to the buyer.

---

## Stacking Rules
- Student and referral discounts don't stack with each other — apply whichever is larger
- Individual-plan discounts (student/referral/annual) don't stack with bundle pricing — a bundle buyer gets the bundle rate, not bundle + student
- **Maximum discount on any bundle: 30%**, no exceptions without Founder/CVO sign-off

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. **Discounting before explaining value** — always lead with cost-per-outcome, not price
2. **Approving a bundle discount without documentation** — always capture seat count + org name
3. **Forgetting non-profit verification** — the 30% rate requires it, no exceptions
4. **Quoting a discount before it's actually approved** — confirm first, quote second
