# Lightforth — Quick Notes

## Sales Tips

### Discovery
- Ask open-ended questions, let them talk — aim for 70/30 listening
- Get a real job posting from them before the demo, not a generic example

### Demo
- The Interview Copilot live-suggestion moment is the close — let the silence sit after it appears
- Answer "isn't this cheating?" before they have to ask

### Closing
- Propose the 14-day trial as the next step, not a future decision
- "When would you like to start?" not "Would you like to start?"

## Competitor Quick Reference

### Teal
- **Strength:** Free tracker, big user base
- **Weakness:** No Auto-Apply, no live interview coaching
- **Our advantage:** Whole funnel through the live interview

### Kickresume
- **Strength:** Resume templates and design
- **Weakness:** Stops at the document
- **Our advantage:** ATS-matched tailoring per job + application + interview support

### Jobscan
- **Strength:** Well-known ATS score
- **Weakness:** Score only, doesn't rewrite for you
- **Our advantage:** Scores AND rewrites AND applies AND preps

### Big Interview / Pramp
- **Strength:** Structured mock-interview practice
- **Weakness:** Practice only, nothing live in the real interview
- **Our advantage:** Copilot works during the actual interview

## Objection Quick Reference

### "Too expensive"
→ "What's one job worth to you? Pays for itself if it gets you hired even two weeks sooner."

### "Isn't this cheating?"
→ "It surfaces your own experience faster — it doesn't invent one for you."

### "Already tried a competitor"
→ "What was missing?" — usually: nothing followed them into the real interview.

### "Need to think about it"
→ "Trial's free, no card. Try it on your next real application instead."

## Talk Track Quick Reference

**Opening:** "Where are you in your search right now — just starting, or already interviewing?"
